AI Tool Adoption — Internal Stakeholder Survey ALY6060
Ritik Shah · Northeastern University

Problem Statement

Organizations rolling out AI tools face a critical stakeholder challenge: employees are often unprepared, undertrained, and uncertain about how AI will affect their roles. This internal survey investigates employee readiness, job-displacement concerns, satisfaction with current AI tools, training adequacy, and preferred AI solutions — providing actionable data to guide a more effective, people-centered AI implementation strategy.

Survey Questions

1
How ready do you feel to adopt AI tools in your current role?Rating scale: 1 (Not ready at all) – 5 (Fully ready)
2
How concerned are you about AI tools replacing parts of your job?Options: Not Concerned · Slightly Concerned · Moderately Concerned · Very Concerned · Extremely Concerned
3
How satisfied are you with the AI tools currently available to you at work?Rating scale: 1 (Very dissatisfied) – 5 (Very satisfied)
4
Has your organization provided adequate training and support for AI tool adoption?Options: Yes · Somewhat · No
5
Which type of AI tool do you think would most improve your productivity and reduce stress?Options: Automation Tools · Data & Analytics Assistants · Writing & Communication Tools · Scheduling & Task Management · Customer Service AI
